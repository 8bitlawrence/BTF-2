# BTF 2

An indie game that combines strategic skill-based PvP with the thrill of RNG-powered pet and fruit collection!

## About the Game

BTF 2 is a competitive PvP experience where players battle against each other to roll unique pets and fruits. Every match is differentâ€”combine skill with luck to build the ultimate team and dominate your opponents.

## Gameplay Features

- **Skill-Based PvP Combat** - Outplay your opponents with strategic decision-making and quick reflexes
- **RNG Pet & Fruit System** - Roll for rare and powerful pets and fruits to enhance your team
- **Dynamic Battles** - Engage in fast-paced PvP matches with real-time action
- **Roguelike Elements** - Build synergies between your collected items across multiple runs
- **Ranked Progression** - Climb the ranks and prove your mastery
